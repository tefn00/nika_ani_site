HR
Heart Rate & HRV
Continuous photoplethysmography (PPG) sensors track your heart rate around the clock. Heart Rate Variability (HRV) analysis reveals your body's stress and recovery state in real time.
Accuracy: ±2 BPM · Sampling: every second
ZZ
Sleep Tracking
Advanced sleep stage detection identifies Deep, REM, and Light sleep cycles. Wake up knowing exactly how restorative your night was with a Sleep Performance Score.
Stages: Deep · REM · Light · Awake
RC
Recovery Score
Each morning, Wellex calculates your Recovery Score (0-100) based on HRV, resting heart rate, and sleep quality. Know precisely when to push hard and when to rest.
Score: 0-100 · Updated every morning
ST
Strain Tracking
The Strain Score (0-21) measures total cardiovascular load throughout the day. Balance your effort with your recovery to optimize long-term athletic performance.
Scale: 0-21 · Exertion + Calorie metrics
